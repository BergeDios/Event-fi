Objetives:

Our project was inspired by the necessity of a way to easily share and find out about events, that didn’t require multiple apps and steps to achieve, these events being work, friends, family or culturally related. Event-fi is a platform created thinking in those porposes, trying to solve the antiqued way for events organization and bringing a new organizational experience with a high level of customization.
